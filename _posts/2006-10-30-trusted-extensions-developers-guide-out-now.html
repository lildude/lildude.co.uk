You can now get hold of the great new Developer's Guide for Trusted Extensions. The Solaris Express version of this guide is now available.

This guide will help developers understand how to take advantage of label manipulation APIs, the Windowing system, interprocess communication and even includes a prototype Trusted Web Guard application.

So go take a look or download a PDF copy, and get developing. We want to see some pretty cool Trusted Extensions developments soon.
