OpenSolaris 2008.11 RC was pushed out late Friday evening, so time to get updating.

One of the most notable bug fixes is the larger than expected memory requirements that RC1 had (bug 3597).

There is also an automated installer image so you can get experimenting with the new automated installer functionality (aka Jumpstart for OpenSolaris) that will become available for the first time with OpenSolaris 2008.11.
