You know open source software has got to be good when rev 22.1 becomes available. In this case it's for one of the longest running UNIX editors - Emacs. Rev 22.1 was released on Saturday. If you're curious, the first release was written and released by Richard Stallman in 1975. Despite it's age, I'm still a Vi/Vim person.
