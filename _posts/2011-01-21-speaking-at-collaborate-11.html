I've just had the fortunate luck to be selected to provide a presentation at Collaborate 11 this year.

The topic:

Sun Support and Patching Strategies - Want to streamline your patching strategy for your SUN systems?

Attend this session, and learn how in a live demo. In addition to a patching strategy overview, this session will walk you through a strategy that includes the following:

  1. how to locate and download patches,
  2. how to see and compare patches currently applied in your environment, and
  3. how to leverage My Oracle Support Communities to resolve your patching questions in a real-time, self-serve manner.

Make the most of your software and support investment as you learn about the tools and resources available to you. You'll leave this session equipped to plan an end-to-end patching strategy for your Oracle Sun systems.

So if you're going, and you read this, pop by and say hello. I promise I'll try not to make it a death-by-slides session.
