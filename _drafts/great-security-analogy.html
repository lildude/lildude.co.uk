> The question is: what more does the customer need?

A can of security spray, which, when applied liberally,
leaves everyone involved with a comfortable level of CYA.
Actually reading a document and taking action involves
an uncomfortable level of free will for which someone
could be blamed later on; a script removes any need
to think and thus any chance of being blamed when things
go wrong.
